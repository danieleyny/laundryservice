Common Questions
About Our Partnership

How do I get started with the partnership?

Simply reach out to us through our contact form, and we’ll guide you through the onboarding process. For faster onboarding, complete our partnership agreement to automatically join our team. We also provide interest letters to your tenants to help you achieve results quickly.

What’s the benefit of partnering with your service?

Partnering with us allows you to offer a highly desirable amenity without any upfront investment. Our laundry service helps you rent units faster and at higher rates, while providing you with an additional revenue stream through our profit-sharing model.

How do I get paid?

Once your first referred tenant starts using our service, we will contact you to gather your Zelle information. You will then receive monthly payments based on the tenant’s laundry usage. If you'd prefer to get paid through a different method, we can discuss and accommodate.

How is the revenue split between us?

We offer a tiered revenue-sharing model based on the number of tenants you refer. The more tenants who join our service through your referral, the higher your equity share grows!

How does your service work for tenants?

Tenants simply leave their laundry outside their door on one of our designated pick-up days, and we take care of the rest. We handle pick-up, washing, folding, and delivery on the scheduled drop-off day. This seamless service is highly valued by tenants, making it a fantastic amenity for your property.

How do I track tenant usage and payments?

If requested, you’ll have access to a management portal where you can track tenant usage, view real-time data, and monitor your monthly payouts. Everything is fully transparent and easily accessible at your fingertips.
