Some common questions
we are often asked

How do I begin using this service?

Check our building list to verify that we work with your property. If so head over to our interest form and submit your information. We will reach out to you with more information and custom laundry bags so that you can start with our service immediately.

How do I pay?

You will receive an invoice on a per use basis. This invoice will be sent to your email and to your text. You may also visit our website payment section to complete your payment.

What is the price for the service?

We provide a price based on your buildings zip code. To determine the price we create a sheet and collect all of the prices for laundry services in the area. We then find the average and provide our customers with a lower per pound laundry price.

When does the laundry get picked up and dropped off?

Certain days and times throughout the week will be selected as pick up and drop off times for each building. Requests for pick up dates may be submitted and will always be considered. If we can add the requested date / time into the pick up schedule then it will be added.

Do I have to be home during laundry pick up / drop off?

No, we provide an option where you may leave your laundry in front of your apartment door prior to your pick up time and we will collect it. Your laundry can also be left in front of your door when its dropped off if you do not believe that you will be home during the scheduled drop off.

Are there any minimums?

We have no minimums! Most locations have a monitary minimum that you must reach in order to receive their per pound price. The minimum is almost always an amount that the average laundry user will not reach. Which forces them to pay more then what they believe they are paying on a price per pound basis.
